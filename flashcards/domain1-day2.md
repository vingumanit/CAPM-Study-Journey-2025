# Day 2 — Flashcards  
## Domain I — Principles, Process Groups, Key Documents

Format: **Term → Definition**

---

**Stewardship** → Acting responsibly, ethically, and as a guardian of resources.  
**Team** → Creating a collaborative, respectful, high-performing environment.  
**Stakeholders** → Engaging individuals/groups who affect or are affected by the project.  
**Value** → Delivering measurable benefits and outcomes.  
**Systems Thinking** → Understanding the project as part of a broader, interconnected system.  
**Leadership** → Guiding the team with empathy, vision, and adaptability.  
**Tailoring** → Adjusting PM methods and tools to the project's context.  
**Quality** → Ensuring deliverables meet required standards.  
**Complexity** → Recognizing and navigating dynamic relationships and uncertainties.  
**Risk** → Identifying and responding to uncertain events.  
**Adaptability and Resilience** → Adjusting to change and recovering from setbacks.  
**Change** → Supporting organizational transformation resulting from project outcomes.

---

**Initiating** → Authorizing the project and identifying key stakeholders.  
**Planning** → Developing plans and **baselines**.  
**Executing** → Performing the planned work.  
**Monitoring & Controlling** → Tracking performance vs **baselines**.  
**Closing** → Finalizing deliverables and documenting lessons learned.

---

**Project Charter** → Formally authorizes the project and grants PM authority.  
**Scope Baseline** → Approved scope: Scope Statement + WBS + WBS Dictionary.  
**Communications Management Plan** → Defines what, how, when, and to whom information is communicated.  

---

End of Flashcards.
